About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is the ultimate hackathon for innovators, coders, designers, and dreamers. Join 300+ participants as you build, learn, and compete for amazing prizes over three electrifying days!

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hacker, HackSphere is your gateway to networking, mentorship, and tech stardom.

FAQ

Who can participate?

Anyone 16+ with a passion for tech! Students and professionals welcome.

Is there a registration fee?

Nope! HackSphere is 100% free for all accepted participants.

Do I need a team?

No team? No problem! You can form teams at the event or join solo.

What should I bring?

Your laptop, charger, and lots of energy! We’ll provide snacks, Wi-Fi, and fun.
